Lower thirds

Name banners, sub-lines, broadcast strips. Every lower third is a three-beat performance: in (1.0–1.5 s), hold (~1.5 s), out (~1.0 s). Below, twelve designs across the corporate / kinetic / broadcast / podcast ranges, all driving the same placeholder "CASEY MORGAN — PRODUCT LEAD" so you can compare the personality of each motion without the words changing.

Lower thirds

Pick the rhythm that suits the show: bars feel corporate, brackets feel editorial, news strips feel broadcast, podcast strips feel social. Hold time is consistent across the set so you can swap between them on the same project without retiming each.

Slide-in bar

Solid accent bar slides in from the left, holds, exits right. The corporate default. AE: position keyframes on a shape layer with ease-in. 32% in / 30% hold / 30% out / 8% rest.
