Shape Layer Tricks

Shape layers scale losslessly, render fast, and have operators no rasterised precomp can do. The Trim Paths + Repeater combo is the backbone of 80% of native-AE mograph.

Trim Paths write-on

Create Shapes from Vector Layer → animate Trim Paths End 0 → 100%. The hand-drawn write-on.

Repeater radial burst

Shape layer + Repeater, 12 copies × 30° rotation. Animate Trim Paths Start + End for expansion.

Repeater receding march

20 copies × (position +40px, scale 95%). Animate Repeater offset for motion-along-path.

Text on a path

Path Options → Path → pick a mask. Text bends along the curve. Classic kinetic typography move.

Pucker & Bloat

Shape operator that morphs a path toward its centre (pucker) or outward (bloat). Organic breathing.

Zig Zag

Converts a smooth path into stepped or wavy version. Distressed edges, electric arcs, scribble lines.

Particles & Advanced

The tools that push past native AE. Plexus for network/constellation looks, particle systems for dust and embers, and feTurbulence for liquid distortion reveals.

Plexus-lite constellation

Points + lines within distance threshold. For AI/data/tech briefs. Full Plexus in AE adds Points from Paths + Noise Effector.

Ember particles

CC Particle World equivalent — dust, embers, atmosphere. Birth rate, gravity, longevity, velocity drift.

Liquid reveal

feTurbulence + feDisplacementMap. Word morphs LIQUID ↔ REVEAL through the distortion peaks. AE equivalent: Turbulent Displace animating Amount.

Film grain loop

Per-frame canvas noise at ~55% opacity over the footage, overlay blend. The analogue warmth pass.

Dust motes in shaft

Slow-drifting particles in a diagonal light shaft. Adds depth and "air" to interior or window scenes.

God rays

Conic-gradient light shafts with screen blend and slow drift. Atmosphere, volume, divine intervention.

Effects & Looks

Finishing moves that ground hero elements and add texture. Cast shadows make flat type feel physical, halftone + kaleidoscope are taste-specific one-shots.

CAST

Shadow Studio look

Ten stacked offsets build a long directional cast shadow — native AE drop shadow is flat, stacked reads as a physical object. Animate the light direction for the sundial sweep.

Halftone

Omino Halftone — clean photo cross-dissolves into a dot-grid reproduction. Prints, zines, Saul Bass-era posters.

Kaleidoscope

Omino Kaleidoscope — one slice of colour clipped into a 45° wedge, then rotated eight times around centre. Spin + bloom in.

Orton glow

Blurred bright copy layered over the footage with screen blend. Dreamy, romantic — the wedding-film bloom.

Duotone

SVG feColorMatrix maps luminance to a two-colour gradient (indigo → amber). Editorial, poster-graphic.
