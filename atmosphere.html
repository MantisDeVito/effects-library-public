Atmosphere

The ambient layer that sells depth. Particles, grain, dust, volumetric rays — the texture that makes a flat comp feel like a space. Everything here is CSS/canvas-faked in the demos; in AE build with native particles, grain plugins, and shape-layer light shafts. For real drop-on-top plates see Overlays.

The ambient layer

Particles, grain, dust, rays. Texture that sells depth — use lightly, atmosphere as accent, not as content. Layer one or two, never all five.

Plexus-lite constellation

Points + lines within distance threshold. For AI/data/tech briefs. Full Plexus in AE adds Points from Paths + Noise Effector.

Ember particles

CC Particle World equivalent — dust, embers, atmosphere. Birth rate, gravity, longevity, velocity drift.

Film grain loop

Per-frame canvas noise at ~55% opacity over the footage, overlay blend. The analogue warmth pass.

Dust motes in shaft

Slow-drifting particles in a diagonal light shaft. Adds depth and "air" to interior or window scenes.

God rays

Conic-gradient light shafts with screen blend and slow drift. Atmosphere, volume, divine intervention.

Blob morph background

Ambient gooey shape that never repeats exactly. Polar coordinates with per-point sine-wave perturbations + slow rotation. Drop behind any content for mood. JS-generated SVG path.

Flow field particles

260 particles drifting through a noise field, canvas 2D with fading trails. Different from Plexus — this is generative flow, not a network graph. Works as a title background or ambient layer.

Weather + environmental

Mood layers that sell where the shot is. Drop on top of footage at low opacity (15–35%) for storytelling — fog for tension, rain for melancholy, sun flare for warmth, snow for stillness. AIIMS Branding / TIWH staple.

Fog rolling in

Three layered horizontal fog bands drifting at different speeds with heavy blur. Volumetric depth, melancholic mood. AE: precomp two fractal noise layers with opacity ramps + slow X-position drift, screen blend over the plate.

Rain on glass

Diagonal streak gradients animating downward + scattered droplet specks. The "looking out a wet window" mood. AE: CC Rainfall plus a CC Vector Blur on a duplicate plate for the glass distortion.

Falling snow

90 flakes falling at varied speeds with subtle horizontal sway. CSS keyframes + per-flake CSS variables for depth (size + opacity + duration vary). AE: CC Snow with depth-of-field, or particle world with size variation.

Wisp smoke

Cigarette-style rising wisp from a glowing source point — four staggered puffs that scale and fade as they rise. Use sparingly; adds noir / contemplative tension. AE: Trapcode Particular emitter with Z-position drift, or precomp turbulent displacement on a soft white shape.

Sun flare reflection

Bright core + horizontal anamorphic streak + secondary ghost orb across-frame. Slow pulse on the streak so it breathes rather than sits. AE: Optical Flares preset, or shape-layer with screen blend + box blur.

Lens dust specks

~40 sub-pixel specks drifting slowly with parallax variation, fading in and out asynchronously. Different from "dust motes in shaft" (which is light-shaft-bound) — these are lens-surface dust, ambient. AE: Trapcode Particular with very low velocity + size jitter.

Heat shimmer

SVG turbulence + displacement map applied to the lower half of the frame, animated baseFrequency for breathing distortion. Desert highway / hot-day exterior. AE: Turbulent Displace on a precomp with masked horizon line.

Grain on anything trying to feel warm. Rays on any interior with a window. Embers for fire, tension, or anything dying. Plexus only when a brief says "AI" or "data". These are texture, not content — layer lightly.
